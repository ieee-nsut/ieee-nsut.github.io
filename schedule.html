“Research is to see what everybody else has seen, and to think what nobody has thought”- Albert Szent Gyorgyi

The most awaited day of IEEE INDICON has arrived, where researchers of IEEE India would be showcasing innovative contributions they documented and bring all of a hope for a promising future. The 17th edition of IEEE INDICON is being hosted by Netaji Subhas University of Technology under Delhi Section & the inaugural ceremony for same will commence from 10th December, 2020 at 10 AM. If you wish to join this ceremony, refer to the document for links. The session will start with three tutorial sessions, and links to separate rooms are given in the document.

IEEE INDICON: IEEE INDICON is the annual flagship international conference conducted by IEEE India Council. With plenary sessions, keynote addresses by reputed academicians, tutorials, workshops, Student Paper contests, industry exhibits and stalls and most importantly, high quality presentations from the best of the researchers in India, no effort is being spared to make INDICON 2020, the best so far. This year IEEE INDICON is being hosted at Netaji Subhash University of Technology.
